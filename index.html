Rules & Scoring
Everything you need to play, score points and win.
How it works
01 Register your team
Click Submit Find and create a team name and password. Share the password with your teammates — up to 8 people. You can also play solo.
02 Find the artifacts
55 wooden boards are hidden across Gdańsk. Each shows a letter and a number. Rare artifacts (teal dot on the Artifacts map) are worth more points.
03 Submit your find
Enter the artifact number, take a photo as proof and hit Submit. The honour system applies. Your find is processed within 60 seconds.
04 Climb the leaderboard
First finder earns 3× the base points. Find all 55 and claim the 1,500 pt completion bonus. The leaderboard updates live.

Winning strategies
Speed Runner
Race to be first on as many artifacts as possible. Every first find earns 3× the base points. Split your team across the city to cover more ground than anyone else.
Rare Hunter
Prioritise the 10 rare artifacts. A first find on a rare earns 90 pts — three times a standard first find. Plan your route using the Artifacts map before you start.
Completionist
Target all 55 for the 1,500 pt completion bonus — the single biggest reward in the game. Even late finds earn base points. Persistence beats speed here.
Q&A
Can I play alone?
Yes. Register any team name and keep the password to yourself. You appear on the leaderboard as a solo player.
Two teams find the same artifact. Do both count?
Yes. Every team that finds an artifact earns points. First earns 3×, second earns 2×. Being second still pays.
What counts as valid proof?
A clear photo of the wooden board showing the letter and order number. The best photo is with you and the artifact 🙂 Photos are compressed on your device before upload.
Can teammates submit from different phones?
Yes. Sign in with the same team name and password on any device. All submissions are credited to the team.
What if an artifact is missing or has been moved?
Report it to the organisers. A missing artifact can be marked as lost and excluded from the completion requirement.
How are ties broken?
The team whose last submission arrived earlier wins.
Is there a submission rate limit?
Each team can submit up to 10 finds per hour. More than enough given the distances between artifacts.
Can the same team submit the same artifact twice?
No. Duplicate submissions are rejected automatically on both device and server.
